# Background

## Smarp — Leader in Employee Communications

Smarp is the leading employee communication platform trusted by global enterprises including Google, Coca-Cola, and L'Oréal. With over 50,000 active users across Fortune 500 companies, Smarp empowers organizations to connect teams, share knowledge, and build stronger company culture.

![fullscreen](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1557804506-669a67965ba0?w=1600&h=900&fit=crop)

## The Challenge — Outdated Design Holding Back Growth

The previous design was built on legacy patterns that no longer served modern workplace needs. Cluttered interfaces, inconsistent components, and outdated visual language created friction across the entire user experience. Navigation was confusing, key features were buried, and the mobile experience was an afterthought.
